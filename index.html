About

Hi, my name is Marc and I am currently working as a DevOps Engineer in one of the largest chemical companies in the world. Here I take care of the following tasks among others:

  • Deploying IT infrastructure components to the Azure Cloud using Terraform
  • Create, maintain and customize CI/CD pipelines in Azure DevOps
  • Create, maintain and develop databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB)
  • Deploy, customize, and maintain applications on Kubernetes-based platforms (Rancher)
  • Create, maintain and customize Kubernetes files (kustomize)
  • Create, maintain and extend Docker resources (Images, Dockerfile, Compose)
  • Integrate and customize security-related analytics and testing into automated deployments
  • Automation of repetitive tasks and simplification of complex processes

After work, my DevOps routine doesn't stop. In my spare time I like to learn new things, especially in DevOps and Full Stack Development topics. I am passionate about implementing new processes and technologies from scratch. I'm also interested in software development which is why i started to learn programming languages and their frameworks.
